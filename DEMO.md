## Quick Start (5 Bullets)
1. **Open the demo URL** - Form loads with 3 input fields
2. **Enter learning objective** - e.g., "Students will understand Newton's First Law of Motion"
3. **Select grade level** - Dropdown 1-12 (default: Grade 9)
4. **Select subject** - Physics, Chemistry, Biology, or Mathematics (default: Physics)
5. **Click "Generate Lesson Plan"** - Waits 5-10 seconds, displays complete lesson plan
